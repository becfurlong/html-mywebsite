My first role in product came after spending two years studying in California. The implementation role developed my collaboration skills and strenghened my ability to work with cross functional teams. My love of product was born.

Since then my roles have generally been part of the operations team helping to implement the product or roll out new features. At the beginning of 2024, I decided to take the leap into learning software development and here we are with my first website project.
